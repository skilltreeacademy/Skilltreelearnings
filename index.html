Privacy Policy – Skilltree Learning

Last updated: January 2026

Skilltree Learning ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy and personal data of users who visit or use our website and services. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you register for or participate in training programs offered through our platform.

By accessing or using Skilltree Learning, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

1. About Skilltree Learning

Skilltree Learning provides a platform where users can select and register for technology training courses, which are delivered in a virtual learning environment. Our services are offered to students, freshers, professionals, and organizations globally.

2. Information We Collect

A. Personal Information

We may collect the following personal details during registration or communication:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number

This information is collected only when voluntarily provided by the user.

B. Learning & Training Data

To deliver training services effectively, we may collect:

  • Course registration details
  • Course progress and participation records
  • Quiz and assessment results
  • Training completion records
  • Certificates issued by Skilltree Learning

C. Payment Information

  • Skilltree Learning offers paid courses
  • Payments are currently accepted via offline methods such as UPI or bank transfer
  • We do not store credit/debit card or bank account details on our servers

3. Information of Minors (Under 18 Years)

Skilltree Learning may provide training to users under the age of 18. In such cases:

  • Registration and participation are assumed to be done with parental or guardian consent
  • We do not knowingly collect unnecessary personal data from minors

4. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Register users for selected courses
  • Deliver training and learning services
  • Track course progress and assessments
  • Issue certificates
  • Communicate training-related updates
  • Send newsletters and important announcements
  • Improve course quality and user experience
  • Meet legal and regulatory requirements

5. Communication & Marketing

We may communicate with registered users via:

  • Email newsletters
  • WhatsApp messages, limited strictly to training-related communication for registered candidates

Currently, users do not have an automated opt-in or unsubscribe mechanism. However, users may request to stop communications by contacting us directly.

6. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

At present, Skilltree Learning:

  • May use basic cookies for website functionality in the future
  • May integrate analytics or tracking tools (such as Google Analytics or advertising pixels) later

If such tools are enabled:

  • They will be used only to understand website usage and improve services
  • No personally identifiable information will be sold or misused
  • Updates will be reflected in this Privacy Policy when implemented

7. Third-Party Services

Currently, Skilltree Learning does not use external third-party platforms such as:

  • LMS providers
  • CRM tools
  • Email marketing platforms
  • Video conferencing tools

If third-party services are introduced in the future, they will be selected carefully and bound by appropriate data protection obligations.

8. Data Sharing & Disclosure

  • We do not sell, rent, or trade personal data.
  • Information may be shared only:
    • When required by law or legal process
    • To protect our rights, users, or platform security
    • With trusted service providers if needed for service delivery (under confidentiality obligations)

9. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect personal data from:

  • Unauthorized access
  • Loss or misuse
  • Disclosure or alteration

However, no system can be guaranteed 100% secure.

10. Data Retention

We retain personal and learning data only as long as necessary to:

  • Fulfill training and certification purposes
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Resolve disputes

Users may request data deletion, subject to legal and operational requirements.

11. International Users

Skilltree Learning serves users in India and internationally. By using our services, you consent to the transfer and processing of your information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

12. Your Rights

Depending on applicable laws (including India DPDP Act and GDPR), users may have the right to:

  • Access their personal data
  • Request correction or deletion
  • Withdraw consent (where applicable)
  • Raise concerns regarding data usage

Requests can be made by contacting us directly.
